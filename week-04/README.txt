BACARDI IOWA LIQUOR MARKET ANALYSIS
=====================================
 
This project analyzes Bacardi's performance in the Iowa liquor market using a SWOT framework. 
The data covers January 2014 through February 2015 and is sourced from the Iowa Alcoholic 
Beverages Division, which controls all spirits sales in the state.
 
DATA USED
---------
Transaction-level sales data across 1,322 stores and all 99 Iowa counties, monthly revenue 
trends for Bacardi and its main competitors (Diageo, Pernod Ricard, Luxco), market share by 
category (rum and vodka), product-level performance by SKU and bottle size, store-level revenue 
trends for the top 10 accounts, and county-level population and revenue per capita data.
